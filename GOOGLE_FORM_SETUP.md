# Google Form Setup

Create the form while signed into:

```text
kashmirsikhconnect@gmail.com
```

## Title

```text
Join Kashmir Sikh Connect
```

## Description

```text
Kashmir Sikh Connect is a purpose-driven community for Kashmiri Sikh students, graduates, professionals, and job seekers.

Use this form to request career guidance, education guidance, mentorship, skill support, or responsible job references. Submissions are reviewed before adding members to keep the community focused and respectful.
```

## Questions

1. Full Name
   Short answer, Required

2. Email Address
   Short answer, Required

3. WhatsApp Number
   Short answer, Required

4. City / Current Location
   Short answer, Required

5. I am a
   Multiple choice, Required
   - 10+2 Student
   - Graduate
   - Professional
   - Job Seeker
   - Mentor / Volunteer

6. Current education / profession
   Short answer, Required

7. Field of interest
   Short answer, Required
   Example: Engineering, Medical, IT, Government Exams, Business, Design, Finance

8. I need help with
   Checkboxes, Required
   - Career guidance
   - Education pathway
   - Skill development
   - Resume review
   - Interview preparation
   - Job / internship leads
   - Mentorship
   - I want to guide others

9. Briefly tell us what guidance you need or how you want to contribute
   Paragraph, Required

10. LinkedIn profile or portfolio
    Short answer, Optional

11. Are you comfortable being contacted on WhatsApp/email for community updates?
    Multiple choice, Required
    - Yes
    - No

12. Community agreement
    Checkboxes, Required
    - I agree to keep discussions respectful, career-focused, and purpose-driven.

## Settings

- Collect email addresses: On
- Link responses to Google Sheets: On
- Response notifications: On
- Confirmation message:

```text
Thank you for joining Kashmir Sikh Connect. Your request has been received and will be reviewed. If approved, you may be contacted through WhatsApp or email.
```
